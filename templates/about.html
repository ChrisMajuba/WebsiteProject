About Active Force!

Force Active is a Health and Fitness company that is meant to help people within a specific location connect with Fitness Instructors and/or book for a training session. The website also proves other services such as Exercise programs and Diet plans. The CEO, Chris Sibusiso Majuba is a BSc in Mathematical Sciences Graduate and a Qualified Personal Trainer, his soul goal is to create a community of healthy individuals working hand in hand and striving for success.
